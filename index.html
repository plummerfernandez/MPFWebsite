M Plummer-Fernandez is a Colombian-British artist with a background in design and engineering. His works configure and entangle computational, social and environmental processes, towards a multiplicity of outcomes ranging from sculpture to installations, to software tools and research.

M PF is also a researcher of new art practices and software automation, having recently completed a PhD at Goldsmiths, London, and having curated group shows of emerging art communities. He runs the blog algopop on everyday algorithmic encounters.
