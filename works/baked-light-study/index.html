Baked light study

2017
Gypsum-based 3D prints, OBJ and texture files baked in Blender


3D modelling applications enable light to be simulated, and to freeze this simulation the rendered light is sometimes 'baked' onto texture files. This project was a study into converting baked light into 3D printed objects. The photograph above is not a render but the physical manifestation with the simulated light and shadows 3D-printed onto the surface of the objects.
