Every Mickey

2017
50.688 x 23.858 x 49.376 cm
SLS Nylon, acrylic paint, 3D files of Mickey Mouse sourced online


Using a 3D file search engine, I searched for every Mickey Mouse. From rather convincing Mickeys to more amateur interpretations, these all clearly breached Disney's infamous mouse copyright. Making a compilation of these circumvents copyright (under US copyright law, a compilation of existing material constitutes a new work of authorship). Further to this, it could be argued that amalgamating all the files into one monstruous multiplicity, disfigures the figure beyond the protected threshold.
