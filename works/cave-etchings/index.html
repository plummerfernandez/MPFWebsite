Cave Etchings

2021
Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Generative Art process in P5js, FXhash platform


Cave Etchings are produced by a generative art system that creates a grid of small lines that vary in angle and thickness, creating interesting textures and moire patterns. The data that guides the system are the hue, saturation, and brightness values taken from unedited AI generated images of caves. The project will be released on FXhash on Thursday 19th July, at 9pm GMT.
